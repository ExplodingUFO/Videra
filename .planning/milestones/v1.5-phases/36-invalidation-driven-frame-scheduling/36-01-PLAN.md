# Plan 36-01

## Goal

Introduce invalidation reasons and a scheduler that can track dirty frames separately from interactive leases.

## Files

- `src/Videra.Avalonia/Rendering/FrameScheduler.cs`
- `src/Videra.Avalonia/Rendering/InteractiveFrameLease.cs`
- `src/Videra.Avalonia/Rendering/RenderInvalidationKinds.cs`

## Acceptance

1. The scheduler can accumulate invalidations and consume them once.
2. Interactive leases keep continuous ticks only while active.
3. The invalidation vocabulary stays internal.
