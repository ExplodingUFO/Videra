# Phase 174 Plan: Scene Delta and Upload Queue Granularity

## Goal

Make retained-entry upload work explicit and priority-aware without widening into a full scheduler rewrite.

## Tasks

1. Add explicit delta change kinds.
   - Replace the one coarse reupload bucket with typed retained-entry changes.
   - Distinguish at least runtime-object-set changes from imported-asset changes.

2. Add queue coalescing and priority ordering.
   - Store one pending upload request per entry id.
   - Pick the next upload by priority plus stable arrival order.
   - Prefer attached/dirty entries on the interactive hot path.

3. Keep rebind and normal uploads on the same queue path.
   - Continue routing backend-rebind dirties and normal entry changes through `SceneUploadQueue`.
   - Avoid a second upload path or special-case executor.

4. Add focused tests.
   - Delta planner emits explicit change kinds.
   - Queue coalesces repeated entry dirties.
   - Attached dirty entries outrank background pending imports during interactive draining.

## Acceptance

- Scene delta distinguishes more than one coarse reupload case.
- Upload queue coalesces repeated dirty work.
- Upload prioritization reflects visible/interactive runtime paths.
- Backend rebind and normal upload paths stay aligned.
