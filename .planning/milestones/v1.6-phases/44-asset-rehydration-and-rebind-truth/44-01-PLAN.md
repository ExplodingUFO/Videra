# Plan 44-01

## Goal

Teach runtime and render world to upload deferred scene objects through the active resource-factory path.

## Files

- `src/Videra.Core/Graphics/RenderWorld.cs`
- `src/Videra.Core/Graphics/VideraEngine.cs`
- `src/Videra.Core/Scene/SceneUploadCoordinator.cs`

## Acceptance

1. Existing `Object3D` instances can be uploaded or re-uploaded through the active resource factory.
2. Wireframe and dependent graphics resources are recreated when deferred objects are rehydrated.
3. `SoftwareResourceFactory` is no longer treated as the steady-state upload path.
