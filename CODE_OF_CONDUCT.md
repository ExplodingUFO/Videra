# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want Videra to be a professional, respectful, and technically serious project.

Participants in this project are expected to:

- Be respectful in discussion, review, and disagreement
- Focus criticism on code, design, and evidence
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory behavior
- Assume good intent, while still holding a high technical bar
- Help keep collaboration useful for maintainers and contributors

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, abuse, threats, or intimidation
- Personal insults or derogatory language
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated bad-faith argument or disruption
- Sexualized language or unwelcome conduct

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately rather than escalating in public threads.
